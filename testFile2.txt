This guide was created as an overview of the Linux Operating System, geared toward new users as an exploration tour and getting started guide, with exercises at the end of each chapter. 
For mor advanced trainees it can be a desktop reference, and a collection of the base knowledge needed to proceed with system and network administration. 
This book contains many rel life examples derivd from the author's experience as a Linux system and network administrator, trainer and consultant. 
They hope these examples wil help you to get  better understanding of the Linux system and that you feel encouraged to try out things on your own.
